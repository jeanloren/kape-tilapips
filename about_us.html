"Here at Kape Tilapips, we don’t only serve coffee — we serve moments. In each cup we brew joy, provide a comforting drink that tastes like home, and create an inviting and warm space where people can just relax, catch up and tell their stories through text on the sticky notes. Whether you are dropping by for that caffeine fix, or staying free of charge to enjoy some free time with friends – we make sure every visit is a special one, and welcomed with lots of heart."
"Our mission is to unite with good cups of coffee, snacks like SIOMAI, and warm hospitality. We believe that foods and drinks are not just sustenance but a medium to connect with one another. "Our mission is to make sure that we are your local café where you come to, when you need warmth, good vibes and taste of home, all of that in one place. We’re not just looking to grow as a brand, we want to grow as a community hub that supports local flavors, values everyone who walks through our door, and builds lasting memories in every sip and every bite."
Kape Tilapips is more than just a café — we serve warmth, stories, and community with every cup.
